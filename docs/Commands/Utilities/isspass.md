# isspass [location]

Tells you when the International Space Station (ISS) is going to pass over <location>.
			

##### Examples

* a!isspass [location] perth
* a!isspass [location] america
* a!isspass [location] new york


##### Aliases

* a!isspasses
* a!isspassover
* a!issover
